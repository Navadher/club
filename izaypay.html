Our Solutions

- Domestic Money Transfer (DMT) Simplified solution to transfer money within the same country, enabling quick and secure transactions to bank accounts or mobile wallets.

- Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS) Secured bank transaction services like cash withdrawal, balance inquiry, and money transfers using an individual's Aadhaar number and biometric authentication.

- Bharat Bill Payments System (BBPS) A unified platform operated by NPCI that simplifies bill payments for utilities, telecom, insurance, and more, offering a secure and seamless experience.

- Payment Gateway for Credit Cards Secured digital infrastructure that facilitates seamless processing of credit card transactions for online and offline payments.

- Cash Management Services (CMS) Offering an efficient and secured automated solutions for managing their cash flow, including collections, payments, and liquidity optimization.

- QR based Static Payment A simple and cost-effective solution for secured and cashless payments through a static QR code.

- Indo-Nepal Transfer A seamless cross-border money transfer service that enables individuals to send funds between India and Nepal with ease. This service leverages digital platforms and trusted banking networks to provide fast, secure, and cost-effective remittances for both personal and business transactions.

- Travel bookings Reserving of flights, hotels, and other travel services online, offering convenience and flexibility in payments.
