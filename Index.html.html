About IEEE SNIST

The IEEE Student Branch at SNIST (Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology) is dedicated to fostering technological advancement, professional growth, and networking opportunities among students. With a diverse and vibrant community, IEEE SNIST plays a key role in organizing workshops, seminars, and hands-on technical sessions to prepare students for their future careers in engineering and technology.

Our Events

  • TechFest 2024: A flagship event called 'ADASTRA' showcasing cutting-edge technology through workshops, project displays, and competitions.
  • WIE Conferencia 6.0 2024: WIE Conferencia (MUN) is a Model United Nations event that empowers women in engineering by fostering leadership, debate, and global collaboration on pressing issues.
  • WIE Star 2024: Aimed at inspiring change and supporting communities through impactful philanthropic initiatives.
  • Webinar Series: Monthly online sessions on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, IoT, and more.
  • Coding Challenges: Competitive programming events for students to enhance their skills.
  • IEEE Day: Annual celebrations with guest lectures, technical quizzes, and fun activities.
